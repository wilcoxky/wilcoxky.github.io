About Me:

Hi my name is Kyle Wilcox, I am currently a fifth year senior at the University of Pennslyvania studying computer science and Chinese. During my first four years of undergrad I was a member of the Varsity football team. In the upcoming year I will be joining Venture for America, a fellowship where you get the oppurtunity to work at start up for 2 years. I am a very driven individual who is always trying to improve himself. If you'd like to chat feel free to contact me!
