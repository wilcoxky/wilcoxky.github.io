Piyinator

Short description of Product

A few reasons why this product is worth using, who it's for and why they need it.

Idea:

Learning Chinese is hard, and for an introductory learner reading a full article is close to impossible. The number of known characters required to even pronounce every line is very high. Coming, from a language with a roman alphabet, my prefered way to read Chinese, as well as many of my peers is through piyin(Chinese romanization). The problem is that articles do not include this and I often annotate the piyin above the characters. This is a painstaking process just to read one article and is not worth it to many people, especially beginners. Thus, the idea of Piyinator was born. Make a tool that would allow users to to have an artcile in Chinese have its piyin annotated automatically for you.

Challenges:

Scraping text from different Chinese artciles, First time creating a Chrome extension, How to properly convert characters to piyin, Formatting document with correct annotation above each word

What I learned

  • Creating Web API
  • How to find patterns when inspecting sites' html
  • Chrome base extension
  • Maintaining correct lnaguage encoding from server to browser, and vise-versa

Tools:

  • Python
  • Flask
  • Jquery
  • Javascript
  • Heroku
